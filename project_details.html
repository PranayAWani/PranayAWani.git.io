About the Project

The AQI Index Monitor is an innovative project aimed at providing real-time air quality data to help individuals and communities make informed decisions for a healthier lifestyle. Our project leverages advanced sensors and cutting-edge technology to monitor various air pollutants and calculate the Air Quality Index (AQI) accurately.

Objectives

  • Provide accurate and real-time air quality data.
  • Increase public awareness about air pollution.
  • Help individuals make informed decisions based on AQI levels.
  • Encourage actions to improve air quality in communities.

Features

  • Real-time air quality monitoring.
  • Interactive and user-friendly mobile application.
  • Detailed reports and historical data analysis.
  • Alerts and notifications for high pollution levels.

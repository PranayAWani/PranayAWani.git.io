Who We Are

We are a dedicated team of environmental enthusiasts and tech professionals committed to improving air quality awareness through innovative solutions. Our AQI Monitoring Application aims to provide real-time air quality data to help you make informed decisions for a healthier lifestyle.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower communities with accurate and accessible air quality information. We believe that knowledge is power, and with the right tools, we can all contribute to a cleaner and healthier environment.

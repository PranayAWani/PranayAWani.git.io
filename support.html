Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How can I monitor AQI in my area? You can use our search function at the top of the page to find the AQI for your location.
  • What does the AQI number mean? The AQI number indicates the level of air pollution. Higher numbers mean more pollution.
  • How often is the AQI data updated? The AQI data is updated in real-time to provide you with the latest information.
  • How can I get alerts for high AQI levels? You can sign up for email alerts through our contact form.
